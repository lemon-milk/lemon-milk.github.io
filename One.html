Yuugen 幽玄 (noun)
The principle of Yuugen enlists that true beauty exists in it's suggestive nature and not in any material feature.

For the longest time, I wanted a project where I had end to end creative control. Shooting and editing client videos is always fun and challenging in their own right; but I wanted to explore what I could achieve creatively when given the freedom. Safe to say that this was my biggest project to date.

With YUUGEN, I had the chance to create something with a bit more cinematic flare than I'm usually used to. Pacing and running themes were much more of a necessity than usual. Establishing a correct and consistant cinematic tone took priority over the more common flashy transitions and quick cut editing style.

This project taught me a lot about my process and flow as an editor. Though, definitely not without it's flaws, I'm happy with the final product. There's still quite a few unstable shots (the opening shot of the Torii gate still burns a hole in my soul), some sound mixing issues, and the overall YouTube compression which irks me to no end. But being 95% happy with a finished product is always better than forever chasing 100% satisfaction with an incomplete project.

For your consideration: I present to you - YUUGEN
