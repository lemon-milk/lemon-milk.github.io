It's not very often you become a godfather. Was more than happy to create something special for my first goddaughter. From the timeline, you can see it's more or less a straight forward video. The hardest parts were remapping some of the audio files to where I felt they would fit best.
I'm happy with how One turned out. Theres a good balace of environment and emotion in the shots. The only things I would change are the Hello Kitty transitions, exporting the project in actual aspect ratio and just tightening the video more in general.
